Public Beta of Eryx TCG: New Factions and Strategies

Calling All Sci-Fi Strategists: Eryx TCG Enters Open Beta with New Content and Platforms!

Attention spacefarers and fantasy warriors! Buckle up and prepare to unleash your inner card battler, because the innovative sci-fi/fantasy card game Eryx TCG is boldly venturing into open beta! This exciting phase marks a significant leap forward for Eryx, introducing a wave of fresh content, novel card effects, and expanded accessibility across multiple platforms.

The Eryx TCG open beta throws open the digital doors to duelists on Android, Linux, and Windows devices (iOS / iPadOS versions will follow). This means more players can join the exhilarating strategic clashes that blend futuristic technology with fantastical creatures. Whether you're a seasoned card game veteran or a curious newcomer, the Eryx TCG open beta offers the perfect opportunity to delve into its deep strategic gameplay and explore the unique blend of genres. So, assemble your deck, refine your tactics, and prepare to dominate the battlefield as Eryx TCG embarks on this new chapter!

Will you succumb to the madness, or will you forge your own dark legend in the face of cosmic dread? 

For those eager to delve deeper, be sure to check out the development notes outlining the latest additions and upcoming features together with concept artworks and designs.
