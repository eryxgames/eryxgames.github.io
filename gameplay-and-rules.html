Gameplay and Rules

ERYXIAN TCG Online Rules

This is an overview of rules of Eryxian TCG pre-alpha (for Android and Windows). You may play solo game modes against AI opponents (multiplayer duel mode on a dedicated server will come in a future).

  • Game for 2 players
  • Players take turns when playing cards on the gameboard
  • Generally, a card need action points as a resource to be played
  • Action points are replenished each turn an number of actions are +1 each consequent turn
  • Player has also HP. If his HP goes to 0 or his cards in the game are destroyed (and there are no resources left in the deck), he loses the game
  • Player can not be attacked directly if a dedicated unit card or an effect card is defending him
  • Game can end in tie if the battle is unresolved or both players are out of resources and no cards ti play
  • ERYXIAN TCG Rules will follow the online multiplayer version release

Card Description

  • Cost/Action points
  • Faction
  • Rarity (how many of instances can be in a deck)
  • Battle stats (Attack, HP)
  • Traits and Affiliations
  • Effects

Factions

In a standard online game cards in custom deck can be from any faction.

Deckbuilding Rules

Only certain number of instances of a card can be present in a player deck, depending on the rarity of a card. Number of cards in a valid  deck for an online game is 20.

Rules differ for Print2Play and Online/Offline versions. 

Full rulebook for ERYX Print2Play Edition will be accessible on the Eryxian project repository.
